Welcome to Igrejas+

Igrejas+ is a platform designed to improve communication between church members and leaders. It provides information about events, services, schedules, and activities. It allows everyone to stay informed through messages, news, and prayer requests.

Application Features

📅 Event Management

Real-time view of church events, services, and activities.

📢 News & Announcements

Important updates and announcements for all members.

🙏 Prayer Requests

A space to share and follow prayer requests from the community.
